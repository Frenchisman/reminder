Welcome to Visual Reminder

Have you ever thought about doing something and then forgot about it five minutes later ?

Or maybe you often hear "Send me an email !" Sadly, we often forget to send it or don't remember what the contents were.

Fear not, Visual Reminder is here.

This lightweight application lets you program a reminder, and sends it the the email address of your choice at the time you choose.

Visual Reminder is usable on mobile as well as on desktop, making it great for any situation. Just whip up your phone and program that reminder.

F.A.Q.

Q: I can't login. PLZ HALP MEEE !

A: That's not a question. If you have forgotten your password, try to reset it, if your account is not active and you have lost the activation email, please use the resend activation key link.

Q: I have not received the activation email, where can I find it ?

A: Check your SPAM folder. If you have not received the email after a few minutes use the resend activation key link.

Q: Is Visual Reminder free to use ?

A: YES !

Q: How many reminders can I send ?

A: You can program up to 5 reminders to be sent on a single day. Once you've reached the 5 limit for a day, you can still program reminders for other days.

Q: Until when can I program my reminders ?

A: You can program a reminder to be sent up to 365 days from now ! Also, we can not send reminders into the past, that feature has not been coded yet.
